About Me

Ever since I was a kid I have found computers fascinating. Our first computer growing up ran on Windows 98. I have fond memories of playing learning games such as "Where in the World is Carmen Sandiego?" and many others. This is where my love for computers began, but it did not cease to exist there. I attended Jacksonville State University to pursue a degree in Computer Science. This is where I was first introduced to programming logic and coding. Though, I did not complete my degree at JSU I learned many fundamentals that carry with me to this day. After many years in the sales workforce I realized that coding was something that I dearly enjoyed and I had a natural talent for. Which lead me to University of Central Florida to pursue a full stack web development course. Within my time at UCF I will become adept with HTML, CSS, Git, Github, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, AJAX, Node, Express, MySQL, Handlebars, Sequelize, Heroku, MongoDB, React. This will be utilizing the MERN stack. I am excited to start a new career, continue building on my programming knowledge, and collaborate with great teams to achieve greatness.
